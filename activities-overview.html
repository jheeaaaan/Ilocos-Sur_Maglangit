Activities & Adventure

Ilocos Sur is far more than a heritage destination, it is a province where you can do things, not just look at them. Ride a horse-drawn kalesa through cobblestone streets, shape clay into traditional burnay jars, paddle a kayak down a quiet river, or cycle past centuries-old churches and tobacco fields. The province offers a rare mix of cultural immersion and outdoor adventure that few destinations in the Philippines can match.

Whether you are an active traveler looking for your next physical challenge or a curious visitor who wants to get your hands dirty learning a traditional craft, Ilocos Sur has an activity waiting for you. These experiences go beyond sightseeing, they connect you with the living culture and natural landscape of the Ilocos Region in a way that no museum exhibit ever could.

What to Do Here

General Activities

The activities available in Ilocos Sur span a wide range, from slow, cultural experiences like watching potters work and joining a heritage cycling tour, to more active outdoor pursuits like river kayaking. What makes these activities special is that most of them are rooted in authentic Ilocano life and tradition rather than designed purely for tourists.

A kalesa ride through Vigan City is not just a gimmick, it is genuinely one of the best ways to navigate the narrow cobblestone streets of the UNESCO Heritage Zone without damaging the atmospheric feel of the place. The sound of hoofbeats on old stone is something you will remember long after you leave.

Pottery making at Pagburnayan connects you directly to one of Ilocos Sur's oldest living crafts, burnay jars have been made using the same foot-kick wheel technique for over 400 years. River kayaking and cycling tours, meanwhile, let you experience the Ilocos landscape at your own pace and on your own terms.

Kalesa Rides
Horse-drawn carriages navigating Vigan's UNESCO Heritage Zone, the most iconic and atmospheric way to explore Calle Crisologo and the surrounding heritage streets.
Pottery Making
Hands-on pottery sessions at Pagburnayan Jar Factory, where artisans have used the foot-kick wheel technique for over four centuries to produce the famous burnay jars.
River Kayaking
Paddling along Ilocos Sur's rivers, a peaceful outdoor activity that offers a completely different perspective on the province's natural landscape.
Cycling Tours
Guided and self-guided cycling routes through Vigan and surrounding heritage towns, passing old churches, tobacco fields, and ancestral houses at a comfortable, scenic pace.
Tip — Combining Activities with Heritage Sightseeing

The best way to experience Ilocos Sur's activities is to combine them with heritage sightseeing in the same day. Start your morning with a kalesa ride through Vigan, spend late morning at Pagburnayan pottery, enjoy lunch along Calle Crisologo, then head out for an afternoon cycling tour through the surrounding barrios. Finish with a sunset at one of the coastal beaches. That is a complete Ilocos Sur day.
