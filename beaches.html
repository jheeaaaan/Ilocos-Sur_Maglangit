Popular Beaches

The beaches of Ilocos Sur may not have the fame of Boracay or El Nido — but that is honestly part of their charm. You get clear water, beautiful sand, dramatic sunsets, and a completely relaxed atmosphere without the crowds or the price tag. Here are the five beaches most worth your time.

Best Beaches

5 Beaches You Should Not Miss

Beach 01

Santiago Cove

Boracay of the North · Santiago

The most famous beach in Ilocos Sur and one of the most beautiful in northern Luzon. Santiago Cove features fine white sand, crystal-clear calm water, and dramatic rock formations framing the cove on both sides. It is often called the "Boracay of the North" but with far fewer crowds and a fraction of the cost. Definitely the number one beach stop in the province.

Beach 02

Vitalis White Sands

Premier Resort Area · Santiago

Also located in Santiago, Vitalis White Sands is a premier beach resort area known for its powdery white sand and quality accommodation facilities. It is the more developed and comfortable option compared to Santiago Cove, making it ideal for visitors who want a beach experience with proper amenities good rooms, clean restrooms, and reliable food service nearby.

Beach 03

Sta. Catalina Beach

Dark Sand Beach · Near Vigan

Unlike any other beach on this list, Sta. Catalina Beach is famous for its fine dark gray and black sand that glitters under the sunlight. Located near Vigan, this beach has calm, blue waters and a quiet, unhurried atmosphere. The unique sand color comes from volcanic minerals washed down from the Cordillera mountains making it one of the most photogenic and unusual shores in Ilocos Sur.

Beach 04

Puerto Beach

Sunset Views · Santo Domingo

Puerto Beach in Santo Domingo is the go-to destination for sunset lovers. Its rustic, unhurried feel makes it a favorite for those who want to sit quietly by the water and watch the sky turn orange and red over the South China Sea. There are no big resorts here just the beach, the breeze, and one of the best sunsets in all of Ilocos Sur.

Beach 05

Ambucao Beach

Calm & Family-Friendly · Cabugao

Located in Cabugao, Ambucao Beach is one of the most relaxed and family-friendly beaches in the province. The water is calm and shallow near the shore, making it safe for children to wade and swim. Coconut trees provide natural shade along the beach, and the local community maintains a clean, peaceful atmosphere that feels refreshingly simple.

💡 Quick Beach Guide

Want white sand and clear water? → Santiago Cove. Want comfort and proper facilities? → Vitalis White Sands. Want something unique and photogenic? → Sta. Catalina Beach. Want the best sunset? → Puerto Beach. Traveling with kids? → Ambucao Beach.
